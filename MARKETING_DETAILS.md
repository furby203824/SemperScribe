# Marine Corps Directives Formatter - User-Focused Marketing Details

## Overview

The **Marine Corps Directives Formatter** is a professional web application designed to streamline the creation of official Marine Corps administrative documents. Built to comply with **SECNAV M-5216.5** standards, this tool eliminates the complexity and manual formatting errors associated with creating Marine Corps Orders (MCO) and Marine Corps Bulletins (MCBul).

## ‚ú® Key Features

### 1. **Comprehensive Document Support**
- **Marine Corps Orders (MCO)**: For permanent directives and policies
- **Marine Corps Bulletins (MCBul)**: For temporary directives with fixed or contingent cancellation dates

### 2. **8-Step Guided Wizard**

The application guides users through a logical, step-by-step process:

1. **Formatting** - Choose document type, letterhead style, and font preferences
2. **Unit** - Select issuing unit from comprehensive database of Marine Corps units
3. **Header** - Configure document identifiers (SSIC, originator code, date)
4. **Optional** - Add references, enclosures, and POC information
5. **Body** - Build document content with structured paragraphs
6. **Closing** - Add signature blocks and closing elements
7. **Distribution** - Specify PCN (Primary Control Number) and IAC (Information Addressee Code) distribution lists
8. **Review** - Preview and generate the final document

### 3. **Extensive Built-in Databases**

- **2,700+ SSIC Codes**: Standard Subject Identification Codes with full nomenclature
- **3,700+ Marine Corps Units**: Complete database of units, installations, and organizations
- **Pre-configured Templates**: Different paragraph structures for MCO vs MCBul documents
- **Distribution Lists**: Standard PCN and IAC codes with descriptions

### 4. **Smart Formatting & Validation**

- **Automatic Alignment**: Precisely calculates header alignment for both Times New Roman and Courier New fonts
- **Real-time Validation**: Checks for required fields, proper formatting, and compliance issues
- **Progress Tracking**: Visual progress bar shows completion percentage
- **Error Prevention**: Prevents advancing steps with incomplete or invalid data

### 5. **Professional Output**

- **Official Seal**: Includes Department of War seal on letterhead
- **Proper Margins**: 1-inch margins with precise formatting (1.5" top on first page)
- **Correct Fonts**: Times New Roman 12pt (body) or Courier New 12pt (specialty documents)
- **Page Numbering**: Automatic page numbering in proper format
- **Headers & Footers**: Compliant headers and footers throughout document

### 6. **Flexible Content Management**

- **Structured Paragraphs**: Build complex, multi-level paragraph structures
- **Dynamic Templates**: Different paragraph types for different document needs:
  - MCO: Purpose, Cancellation, Background, Action, Reserve Applicability, etc.
  - MCBul: Situation, Mission, Execution, Administration & Logistics, Command & Signal
- **Copy Management**: Track and manage multiple copies with proper distribution

### 7. **User Experience Features**

- **Visual Step Navigation**: Click on completed steps to review and edit
- **Draft Saving**: Save work in progress and resume later
- **Mobile Responsive**: Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
- **No Installation Required**: Web-based application accessible from any browser
- **Instant Download**: Generate professional .docx files ready for distribution
